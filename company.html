About the Company

Keydar is a digital studio focused on building interactive products, fantasy universes, digital communication systems, and long-term creative projects.

The company operates in the following areas:

  • Game development
  • Worldbuilding and narrative design
  • Digital content production
  • Automation and communication tools
  • Creative and technical project development

Keydar is currently developing an ecosystem that includes a game universe, lore materials, books, and related digital services.
