The World of Keydar

Keydar is a fantasy universe shaped by ancient forces, divine conflict, evolving civilizations, and large-scale historical change.

The world includes:

  • Distinct races, kingdoms, and factions
  • Gods, ancient powers, and belief systems
  • Conflicts shaped by history, politics, and survival
  • Locations designed for stories, exploration, and gameplay

This universe is being developed as the foundation for future games, books, and long-term interactive projects.
